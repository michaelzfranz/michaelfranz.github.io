Projects

ION Propulsion Project

The ION Propulsion Project is coordinated by Knights Experimental Rocketry, which is a UCF recognized RSO. The goal of this project is to design, construct and test an ION thruster. I am currently developing a heater coil and PSU for the neutralizer cathode.

Dark Current

I am currently assisting Professor Phelps in researching the characteristics of dark current in various integrated components. The goal of this research is to establish an obersvable relationship between dark current and temperature, light, and background radiation.
